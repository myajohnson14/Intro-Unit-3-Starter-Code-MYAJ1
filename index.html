Day in a life

I wake up at about 6:30 - 7am, and lay on bed for about 5 minutes. Then I get up and shower. Then I decide what outfit I am going to wear and if i should eat breakfast .

Since I ate I feel more energized. I finish getting ready and leave the house.

Since I didnt eat I still feel tired. I slowly get ready and leave the house a little late.

I leave the house and go meet my friends at the train. We go to school and I have a good day !

I finally leave the house and meet my friends. I get to school and im super tired. My head starts hurting and I sleep all day.
